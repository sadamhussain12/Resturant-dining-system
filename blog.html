Unveiling Our New Seasonal Menu

Oct 10, 2025 · Culinary · By Chef Amir

Chef Amir
Head Chef · Alumination
Reading time: ~4 min

A fresh chapter for autumn

This autumn we’re celebrating seasonal produce and local growers. The menu focuses on warm textures, smoked notes, and bright finishes — all designed to pair with our curated wine list.

Local sourcing & inspiration

We visited nearby farms and hand-selected produce that inspired the dishes you’ll see. Expect root vegetables, preserved citrus, and a renewed emphasis on balanced savoury-sweet combinations.

“We design dishes that make our guests linger — not just because of taste, but because of memory.”

Chef Amir

Signature dishes to watch

  • Charred autumn squash with smoked pecan crumble
  • Seared seabass, saffron velouté and braised fennel
  • Pear & blue cheese tart with honeyed walnut

We rotate items monthly — follow our journal for announcements, tasting nights, and behind-the-scenes menus.
